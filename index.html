About KTL

Leading textile manufacturer with a commitment to quality and ethics

Our Story

Kattali Textile Ltd. is an export-oriented garment manufacturer based in Chattogram, Bangladesh. We focus on consistent quality, ethical production, and dependable delivery schedules for global brands.

Our Mission

We specialize in high-quality textile manufacturing with comprehensive services from design to delivery. Our state-of-the-art facility operates under strict quality control standards.

Our Services

Comprehensive manufacturing solutions for global brands

Certifications
