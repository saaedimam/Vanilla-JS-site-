About KTL

Leading textile manufacturer with a commitment to quality and ethics

Our Story

Kattali Textile Ltd. is a Bangladesh-based apparel manufacturer serving global brands with reliable, compliant, and on-time production. With 12 production lines and 850+ team members, we maintain ISO 9001 quality systems and CTPAT security standards.

Technology & Innovation

We're investing in real-time production visibility through our in-house tech initiative (StitchOS/TextileTrack). This modernization includes RFID and real-time tracking to cut delays and improve target adherence.
