About Kattali Textile Ltd.

Kattali Textile Ltd. (KTL) is a leading export-oriented apparel manufacturer based in Chattogram, Bangladesh. With 12 production lines and over 850 skilled workers, we serve major markets including the USA, Canada, and the United Kingdom.

Our Capabilities

We specialize in high-quality textile manufacturing with comprehensive services from design to delivery. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with modern machinery and operates under strict quality control standards.

Certifications & Compliance

KTL maintains ISO 9001 certification and C-TPAT compliance, ensuring the highest standards of quality management and supply chain security for our international clients.
